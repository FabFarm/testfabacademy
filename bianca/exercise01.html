The pukeanator:

For my final project I decided on developing a very necessary Fab Machine. A automatic cat feeder. I hope this machine will take some of the burden of fab Managers free from feeding their cats or over feeding their cats, but this won’t happen because everything will happen automatically.

Front and side of the Pukeanator

Inspired and based on the already existing fab academy project (CATS) by Toni F. Barreiros, it will have a screen on the front that shows the time it is feed and the quantity of food on the food bowl, it will also have a mini scale to weight the food bowl so it won’t over feed the cat.
